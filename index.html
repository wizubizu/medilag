The Research Management Office (RMO) of the College of Medicine, University of Lagos was created to advance the College’s capacity to actualize her strategic mission of correct positioning as a world class research intensive Medical School.

The RMO is to value and support the research efforts of all our faculties for development of an effective research infrastructure supported by appropriate human and physical resources, to facilitate research collaborations in prioritized areas and strengthen our connections with external stakeholders and partners.
